Cláudio Danadinho (1970)

Mestre Claudio Danadinho is a highly respected capoeira master and musician who is known for his contributions to the art form. He was born in Bahia, Brazil, in 1956 and began practicing capoeira when he was just six years old. He trained under some of the most renowned masters of his time, including Mestre João Pequeno and Mestre Pastinha. In the 1970s, Mestre Claudio Danadinho became one of the founding members of the Grupo Senzala de Capoeira, which is now one of the most well-known capoeira groups in the world. He has since become a leading figure in the capoeira community, and his teachings have influenced countless students over the years. One interesting fact about Mestre Claudio Danadinho is that he is also a talented musician. He has released several albums of capoeira music, including "Senzala de Capoeira" and "Canto de Capoeira."
