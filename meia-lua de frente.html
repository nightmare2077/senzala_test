Meia-lua de frente

To perform the meia-lua de frente kick, the Capoeirista starts by standing with one foot forward and the other foot behind. The front foot is then lifted and swung in a circular motion towards the opponent's head or torso. The kick is executed with the ball of the foot or the heel, depending on the style of Capoeira being practiced.

The meia-lua de frente kick is often used as a defensive move to create distance between the Capoeirista and their opponent. It can also be used as an offensive move to strike the opponent and set up other attacks.
