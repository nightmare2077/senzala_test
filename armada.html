Armada

To perform the Armada, the Capoeirista starts by standing with one foot forward and the other foot behind. The front foot is then lifted and swung in a circular motion while the body rotates, allowing the kicking leg to come around and strike the opponent with the heel.

The Armada can be used both defensively and offensively, as it can be used to create distance from the opponent or to strike them and set up other attacks. It is often used in combination with other kicks and movements to create complex and fluid sequences.
