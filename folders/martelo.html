Martelo

Martelo is a striking kick used in capoeira. It involves a circular motion of the leg, striking with the heel or the ball of the foot. The kick can be executed from various positions, including the ginga, negativa, and cocorinha.

The martelo is a powerful and versatile kick that can be used both defensively and offensively in capoeira. It can be used to attack the opponent's legs, torso, or head and can also be used to deflect incoming attacks.
